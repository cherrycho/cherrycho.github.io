Design Mockup To Web Page

Posted on 03/03/2024


   Due: 28/03/2024


The lab report assignment serves as a pivotal exercise in bridging conceptual design with practical implementation within the realm of web development. Its core objective is to equip students with the essential skills and methodologies necessary to transform a static design mockup into a dynamic and interactive web interface. Students are presented with the challenging yet rewarding task of harnessing the power of HTML, CSS, and potentially other cutting-edge web development technologies to breathe life into a provided design prototype. Through meticulous analysis and thoughtful interpretation of the mockup's visual cues, layout structures, and stylistic elements, students embark on a journey of creative problem-solving and technical ingenuity.
